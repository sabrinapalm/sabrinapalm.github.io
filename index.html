About me

Hi!

I'm Sabrina, a 29 year old Front-End Developer student at EC Education, Gothenburg, Sweden. I'm a sucker for minimalistic design and I believe in simple but functional websites and applications. Since I'm very curious on how to achieve and solve different things in my code I spend most of my time using W3Schools, MDN, Stack Overflow and probably have a gazillion Google searches.

When time is offered I study courses on Udemy wich is a great place that offers online courses for a reasonable price in various topics. The ones I'm focusing on right now is the Modern JavaScript courses.

What about back in time? At about 15 years of age, I began to develop my creative side, bought my first camera to use for photography, edited my pictures using Adobe Photoshop. After a few years I also found interest in text communication, graphic communication and webdesign.

At this point in my life I feel that I want to be able to use my creative side while building functional websites. Just the thought of being able to create something completely from scratch using code excites me a lot. That's the main reason I chose to start studying to become a front-end developer.

I'm going to do my first internship, I'm looking for a fun company or web agency to get started in. Are you interested in having someone who is willing to learn, develop and also have an eye for design, do not hesitate to contact me!
